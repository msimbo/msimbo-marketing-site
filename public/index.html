Frequently Asked Questions

Is this really free?

Yes — 100% free for all participants. The program is funded through grants and institutional support. There are no tuition fees, no income share agreements, and no hidden costs. Wraparound support — including MBTA passes, meals, loaner laptops, and emergency funds — is also available as needed.

Do I need a tech background?

No. This program is designed for career changers. If you can use email and a web browser, you have enough to start. We teach everything from the ground up.

Do I need a college degree?

No. We use skills-based admissions. What matters is your motivation, your commitment to the 10-week schedule, and your readiness to learn.

What kind of jobs will I qualify for?

RevOps Analyst, CRM Administrator, Marketing Operations Specialist, Sales Operations Analyst, Business Operations Associate, and Revenue Analyst. Entry-level salaries in Boston range from $55,000 to $70,000.

What's the weekly time commitment?

Monday through Wednesday in-person at ULEM in Boston (10 AM–4 PM), plus remote work on Thursdays and Fridays.

Will you help me find a job after the program?

Yes. Career support is built into every week of the program — not just the end. You'll build a portfolio, practice interviews with real hiring managers, and present at a Demo Day attended by employer partners. We continue providing placement support for 90 days after graduation.

When does the next cohort start?

Cohort 1 launches May 4, 2026 with 15 seats. We plan to run additional cohorts throughout the year. Attend an info session to learn more and get early access to the application.

I work full-time. Can I still do this?

The in-person days (Mon–Wed, 10 AM–4 PM) do require daytime availability. If your current schedule doesn't allow that, register for an info session and we can discuss options — including waitlisting for a future cohort with different scheduling.

What if I can't make this cohort?

We plan to run additional cohorts throughout the year. Register for an info session or submit an application and let us know your availability — we'll keep you in the loop for future start dates.

What does the application process look like?

It's straightforward: submit a short application, complete an online assessment (30–60 minutes), then have a one-on-one interview with our team. No resume required. See the full step-by-step breakdown above.
